Operation Gekkou - Reborn is an officially sanctioned fan game based around the game Guns GirlZ. This is the sequel to Operation Gekkou - Retro. The project was done internationally and I was involved in the under the alias JKCrafted. I wore lots of hats in the development, manager, designer, programmer, writer, QA, marketer, and more. Reborn was built completely from the ground up by me alone, further optimising things, adding unique features we couldn't do before, and making development easier overall.
