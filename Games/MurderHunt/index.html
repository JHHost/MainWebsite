Murder Hunt is a demo of a game created for a mock funding pitch my team was doing as apart of our Bachelor Degrees at SAE University. The systems were all created based on full research and were developed in the most optimal way to showcase how it tied in and how it could be understood by investors. We all partook in the research and development, and since it is all done if we wished we could actually escalate to a real life pitch for development to make it a full game.
