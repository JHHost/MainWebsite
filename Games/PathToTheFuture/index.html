Path To The Future is a demo of a 3d world grid based path finding system I built from cratch solo in my second year at SAE University as apart of my Associates Degree. The core system is based around the logic of the A Star Algorithm, but is adapted in multiple ways to achieve more functionality and a smoother experience for the developer and players.
I also implemented a fully customisable controller for the AI, allowing new logic and tasks to be fed into it, allowing for even more functionality.
