NEEDA History Investigation Series is a vertical slice game demo I developed in my second year at SAE University as apart of my Associates Degree in an attempt to pitch and make a serious game focused around learning history.
My main role was that of the programmer lead, where I took on most of the development and lead our other programmer in how to progress. Difficulties during development with team members meant I stepped up to do most of the programming, debugging and misc changes that needed to be done to make the slice complete.
Fun fact, the term "Needa" can be used for so many puns, you'd needa calculator to count.
