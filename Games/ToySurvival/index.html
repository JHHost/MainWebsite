Toy Survival is a game prototype developed during my first year at SAE University as apart of my degree. The project started out as an existing demo which I joined the team of and helped expand. I was in charge of programming, source control, debugging and overseeing development. All the core systems and logic were reworked and built by me to work with our new vision.
