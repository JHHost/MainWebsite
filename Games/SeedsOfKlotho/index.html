Seeds of Klotho was my team's submission to the thatgamecompany × COREBLAZER GAME JAM 2025, with the theme Generosity. The game is focused around collecting seeds in space to gain powers and repopulate the planets.
I was the team's programmer and made the complex logic we had for our vision work.
