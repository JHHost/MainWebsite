Cat Carnival is a micro game collection developed during my first year at SAE University as apart of my degree. I was in charge of programming, source control, debugging and overseeing development. I also took part in developing some of the micro games, specifically the Fishing and Balloon Darts games.
Once the project was completed on the University side I took to completing it and porting it to mobile on my own in my own time. Once this was done I ensured all the rights holders were fine with me publishing it, and took care of everything involved in publishing it to the Google Play Store.
