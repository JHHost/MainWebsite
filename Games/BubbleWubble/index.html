Bubble Wubble was my team's submission for Global Game Jam 2025 with the theme Bubble. It is a minigame collection using traditional minigames that were shifted to make them fit bubbles, including Snake, Asteroids, and Flappy Bird.
Pretty much all of us in the team were programmers, which led to us learning a lot from each other and creating systems heavy but not too complicated games.
The games turned out quite well and fun so I still play them every now and then.
