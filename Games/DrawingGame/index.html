The title says it all and that is very intentional. Online Drawing game was developed solo in my second year at SAE University as apart of my Associates Degree. It makes use of Unity's networking capabilities to give a smooth and connected drawing experience that is really easy to use.
It has been quite popular at showcases it has been in, keeping people's attention and bringing great artistic visions to life.
